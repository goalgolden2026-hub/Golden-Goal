# Security Policy

Golden Goal is committed to ensuring the safety and security of our Web3 prediction platform, our users, and our token ecosystems.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in our codebase, smart contracts, or infrastructure, please report it to us immediately. 

**Do NOT open a public GitHub issue.** Instead, follow our private disclosure channel:

- **Email**: Send detailed vulnerability reports directly to [security@goldengoalsol.com](mailto:security@goldengoalsol.com).
- **Format**: Include a clear description of the vulnerability, step-by-step instructions to reproduce it, a proof-of-concept (PoC) if applicable, and potential impact.

We will review your submission and respond within **24 hours** to coordinate a fix.

## Disclosure Policy

To protect our community, we follow a responsible disclosure policy:
1. We request that you give us reasonable time to investigate and patch the issue before making it public.
2. We ask that you do not target or exploit user funds during your investigation.

Thank you for helping keep Golden Goal secure!
